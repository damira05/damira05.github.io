About me

My name is Damira and I am 18 years old. I was born in the city of Bishkek which is the capital of Kyrgyzstan. I decided to apply to Algonquin College in 10th grade and now I am finally studying here as I dreamed of. I flew to Canada alone and miss my family a little, but I think I made the right decision.

Studying abroad was not my only dream. When I started going to the FD dance studio in my country, I had a dream of becoming a professional dancer. This dream is still with me. I want to perform on a huge stage and show people incredible and exciting performances. Before, all my dreams seemed impossible to me, but as I grew up, I realized that such thoughts were stopping me.

We ourselves don not know what we can... we just need to forget the word “impossible.” -Vera Kamsha
